Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sourdough starter
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Dissolve the starter in the lukewarm water.
  2. Add the salt to the flour.
  3. Add the water and sourdough starter.
  4. Mix well, before letting it ferment for 3 hours.
  5. Shape the loaf.
  6. Let it rise to double the size.
  7. Bake for 50 minutes in preheated oven at about 230 degrees celsius.
